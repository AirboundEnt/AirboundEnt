The Steppin Stones are a rock n’ roll band based out of Charleston, SC. Formed by lead member, Hannah Wicklund, the band started off as a classic rock cover band 9 years ago. Since then, the trio has recorded two albums, ‘Handle Me’ & ‘ Looking Glass’.
The Steppin Stones:
Guitar/Vocals- Hannah Wicklund
Bass/Vocals- Chris Gingrich
Drums- Justice Ian Jones
